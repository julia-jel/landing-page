About us

DrinkDrankDrunk Records is a comfortable music recording studio in the downtown of St Petersburg.

We are placed at: Sadovaya metro station, Kaznaсheyskaya st 2, apt 18.

The sound engineer is an experienced musician and vocal producer. He is also a former member of numerous Russian bands, such as Cheshire Strangler (new wave), Pinkshinyultrablast (shoegaze), Supervitesse (psychedelic pop) and SЫN (Russian post-punk).

The studio is well equiped and has everything for fruitful music creation, including tea, coffee, non-alcoholic beers, books and a rumbling British shorthair.

To book a recording session, please, send a request to vk.com/dddrecords or call +7 (952) 395-50-44.
